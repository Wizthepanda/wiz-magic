WIZXP respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform and connect your Google account.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we request read-only access to your YouTube account using the scope:

  • youtube.readonly — lets us view public video watch history and engagement data to verify you’ve watched content and to award XP on our platform.

We do not:

  • Post, modify, or delete YouTube videos or channel data.
  • Access private messages, upload videos, or manage your account.

We may also collect:

  • Your email address (from Google sign-in) for account management.
  • Engagement stats (likes, views, comments) to award XP.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Verify video-related quests are completed.
  • Award XP and unlock rewards based on engagement.
  • Improve our services and user experience.

We never sell or share your personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely in Firebase with encryption.
  • Access is limited to authorized team members.
  • We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy and OAuth verification requirements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data except:

  • When required by law.
  • With your consent.
  • With trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting) to operate our services.

5. Your Choices

You can revoke our access at any time via your Google Account Security Settings: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
